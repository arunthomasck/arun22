Frequently Ask Questions

How do I open an account with your bank?

Opening an account with us is easy! You can start the process online by visiting our website and filling out the online account application form. Alternatively, you can visit one of our local branches, and our friendly staff will assist you in person.

Can I schedule automatic bill payments through your bank?

Absolutely! Our online banking platform allows you to set up automatic bill payments, making it easy to manage your monthly expenses and avoid late fees.

How can I reach your customer support team for assistance?

You can reach our customer support team through various channels, including our toll-free hotline, email, or by visiting one of our local branches during business hours. We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.

How can I transfer money to another account, both within and outside the bank?

You can easily transfer funds between your accounts or to external accounts through our online banking platform. Log in to your online banking account, select the "Transfer Funds" option, and follow the on-screen instructions. For external transfers, you'll need to add the recipient's account details.

What is the process for reporting fraudulent activity or unauthorized transactions on my account?

If you suspect any fraudulent activity or unauthorized transactions on your account, please contact our fraud department immediately at our dedicated hotline. We will assist you in securing your account and initiating an investigation.

What are the bank's hours of operation for in-branch services?

Our branch hours may vary by location, so we recommend visiting our website for the most up-to-date information on branch hours and locations. You can also contact our customer support team for assistance with specific branch-related inquiries.

Do you offer mobile banking services?

Yes, we offer mobile banking services. Download our mobile banking app from your device's app store to access your accounts, make transactions, and manage your finances on the go.

What steps should I take to update my contact information, such as my address or phone number?

To update your contact information, please visit our website and log in to your online banking account. Navigate to the "Profile" or "Settings" section, where you can make the necessary updates. You can also visit one of our branches and provide the updated information in person.
